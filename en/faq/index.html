FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions about our products and services

首页/FAQ
What products does Ruijian manufacture?
We manufacture API 5CT oil casing (114.3-273mm), tubing (48.3-114.3mm), drill pipe, drill collar, seamless pipe, couplings and short joints. Grades include J55, N80, L80, P110 and L80-13Cr stainless steel.
Are your products API certified?
Yes. Our products are manufactured to API 5CT standards. We are certified under ISO 9001, ISO 14001, and ISO 45001 management systems.
What is your annual capacity?
Annual capacity: oil casing 150,000 tons, upset tubing 30,000 tons, couplings 500,000 pieces. We operate 4 automated production lines and 1 intelligent upsetting/coupling line.
Do you accept custom orders?
Yes. We offer custom manufacturing for non-standard sizes and special connections. Contact our sales team with your requirements.
What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
MOQ for standard products is typically 50-100 tons, depending on specifications and grades. Custom products MOQ is determined by process evaluation.
Do you export? What payment terms are accepted?
Yes. We export globally through our Tianjin sales center to Middle East, Africa, Central Asia and more. Payment: T/T, L/C and other international terms.
Where are your factory and sales center located?
Manufacturing base: Shuangyashan, Heilongjiang. Sales center: Junliangcheng Industrial Park, Dongli District, Tianjin.
How can I get a quote?
Submit an inquiry through our Contact page, or call +86 18618191088 (Kevin). Provide specifications, grade, quantity and delivery requirements. We respond within 24 hours.
