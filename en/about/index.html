Company Overview

Heilongjiang Ruijian Petroleum Equipment Co., Ltd. was established in 2022, located in Shuangyashan City, Heilongjiang Province. We are a high-tech enterprise specializing in the R&D, production and sales of oil country tubular goods (OCTG).

The company operates 4 automated OCTG production lines and 1 intelligent upsetting/coupling line, with processing OD range from 48.3mm to 273mm. Available steel grades include J55, N80, L80, P110 and L80-13Cr stainless steel. Annual capacities: oil casing 150,000 tons, upset tubing 30,000 tons, couplings 500,000 pieces.

In November 2025, Ruijian Petroleum Equipment (Tianjin) Co., Ltd. was established in Tianjin's Junliangcheng Industrial Park, serving as our global sales and export center.
