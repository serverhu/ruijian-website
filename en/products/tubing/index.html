High-Quality Tubing for Optimal Production Performance

Product Overview

Tubing is the conduit through which oil, gas, and injection fluids are transported during production operations. Ruijian Petroleum manufactures API 5CT certified tubing with outer diameters from 48.3mm to 114.3mm, in grades J55, N80, L80, and P110. Available in Non-Upset (NUE), External Upset (EUE), and premium connection configurations. Our tubing is designed for reliable performance in demanding production environments.
