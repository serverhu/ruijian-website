Product Overview

Oil casing is a critical component in oil and gas well construction, used to support the wellbore walls, isolate formations, and ensure safe drilling operations. Ruijian Petroleum manufactures API 5CT certified casing in grades J55, K55, N80, L80, P110, and L80-13Cr stainless steel, with outer diameters ranging from 114.3mm to 273mm. Connections include Short Thread (STC), Long Thread (LTC), Buttress (BTC), and premium connections. Our products are widely used in onshore and offshore oil & gas fields, geological exploration, and geothermal wells.
